How the Cubs broke the 108-year drought in 2016

2022-04-21

108 years.

That’s how long it had been with since the Chicago Cubs won their last MLB World Series. After over a century, the “Lovable losers” knocked off the Cleveland Indians to take the series 4-3 and hoist the ultimate prize.

But how did that do it?

Sure they had the NL MVP and Cy Young in Kris Bryant and Jake Arrieta. But baseball is a team sport, so let’s take a depper dive into the Cubs championship run.
